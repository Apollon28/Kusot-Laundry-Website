Frequently Asked Questions

What is KUSOT LAUNDRY?

KUSOT LAUNDRY is a website that aims to minimize the task of
doing laundry and making it easy on one click. With this website,
the users can easily access the specific laundry shop and avail their
services whether it is for pickup or queuing for self-service. The admin
on the other hand can monitor the business' revenue and transactions report.

Who can avail our services?

Everyone can avail the services of KUSOT LAUNDRY.

Is there a delivery charge?

YES! An additional delivery fee shall be added to your total bill.

How much does our services cost?

The rates of the services that this website varies depending on the services
you wanted to customize or avail. You can check the prices in the checking
out pages before you proceed in confirming your order.

Are there any upcharge fees?

NO! The total price that appears in your checking out bill will be the total
price of the availed service. Also, the website will also display a breakdown
of your purchase for transparency.
